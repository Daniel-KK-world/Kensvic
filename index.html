Know of the benchmarks for nominations.

Eligibility to nominate

The general public is eligible to nominate potential award winners in each sector. Both self-nominations and third part nominations are allowed. However each person is only allowed one set of nominations in which the person nominates ONE person for each sector. In the event that multiple sets of nominations are made by one person all of those nominations would be disqualified.

Who Can Be Nominated

Only CEOs of certain privately owned or state owned enterprises can be nominated. This includes CEOs of government institutions and agencies other than SOEs as well as not for profit organizations such as NGOs and religious organizations. In order to forestall excessive diffusion of nominations, eligible nominees must be CEOs of enterprises
